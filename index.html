About Me

Hello World! I am very excited to start my coding journey. I never thought that I would become a developer but here I am!
I grew up in Mexico City where I spent most of my childhood. My dream was to become a professional Soccer Player (like most kids in Mexico). However, that dream came to an end after getting a knee injury. That's when I had to go to school and study a carreer :(
I chose Hospitality Managemant since I'm a people person, love hosting and appreciate good food and wine. After graduating, I opened and managed a Mexican/American fusion restaurant for 3 years, it was delicious! After selling the restaurant I moved to San Francisco and I've lived here since then, that was 12 years ago. I managed some high end Restaurants and Banquet Facilities throughout the Bay Area, my favorite was The Top of the Mark at the Mark Hopkins Hotel. After 7 years of this work, I decided it was time to persue a different career that would offer a better work/life balance.
I enrolled in a Software Quality Assurance Program and started my Software Engineering Career 7 years ago! I have to admit, it was pretty hard to transition into a new career, but it was best decission I ever made! I am now trying to work on improving my technical skills so I can get a more senior position, or even better, decome a Full-Stack Developer!
